# MyPathAI

**AI-powered course and pathway planner that helps students and families navigate high school with clarity.**

---

## Overview

**MyPathAI** is a modern web application designed to simplify high school course planning for **students (8th–12th grade), parents, and counselors**.

It transforms complex course catalogs and handbook information into an **interactive, easy-to-understand experience**.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎓 Pathway Explorer

* Explore all endorsement pathways (STEM, Business & Industry, Public Services, Arts & Humanities, Multidisciplinary)
* Understand requirements, rules, and course options

### Interest-Based Matching

* Select student interests (coding, healthcare, business, etc.)
* Get recommended pathways aligned to those interests

### 🗺️ Grade-by-Grade Planner

* Planning guidance from 8th through 12th grade
* Milestones, watchouts, and recommended focus areas

### Handbook-Grounded AI Assistant

* Answers questions using **only official handbook data**
* Topics include:

  * Graduation requirements
  * Class rank & GPA
  * K/AP entry criteria
  * Dual credit eligibility
  * Course prerequisites
* If information is not available, users are directed to contact a counselor

### Course Highlights

* Key courses with prerequisites, grade levels, and pathway alignment

---

## Tech Stack

* React (TypeScript)
* ShadCN UI
* Framer Motion
* Custom handbook-based retrieval logic

---

## Problem It Solves

High school course planning is often:

* confusing
* buried in PDFs
* difficult for students and parents to interpret

**MyPathAI solves this by:**

* structuring handbook data into a clear UI
* enabling guided exploration of pathways
* helping students make informed decisions early

---

## Design Philosophy

* Clean, modern, student-friendly UI
* AI as a guide (not a replacement for counselors)
* Strict grounding in handbook content
* Built for both students and families
